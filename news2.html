Uzbekistan national football team to hold friendly match against Belarus

22:31/03.12.2019 (0) comments

In February 2020, the national team of Uzbekistan will hold a friendly match with the team of Belarus. The website of the Belarusian Football Federation reported.

The match will take place on February 23 in the UAE.

It should be noted that the national team of Uzbekistan is on top of Group D with 9 points as part of the qualifying stage of the 2022 World Cup. Saudi Arabia is in the second place by a margin of one point.

In the next match of the qualifying round, which will be held on March 31, the national team of Uzbekistan will play against Yemen.
